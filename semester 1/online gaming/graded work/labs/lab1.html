My game

Story

You are brought into this world by Juno, the goddess of marriage. The gods have disrespected her for the last time.

Hercules was created to appease her, but you... you are the anti-Hercules. You are not Hera's hero. You are Hera's wrath. You are Herahymos.

Gameplay

The gameplay will work as a mix between Dark Souls, Furi and Hyperlight Drifter. Utilising swordfighting, gunplay, blocking and evading.

The enemies consist mostly of bosses, but smaller monsters will provide a constant threat to the player.

You will travel between dimensions fighting gods in their own domain. Claiming the beautiful land of gods and montsers in Hera's name.

Each peice of weaoponry or armour will have their own special characteristics. Statistics will still be present such as damage and defence, but they will be very straight forward.
